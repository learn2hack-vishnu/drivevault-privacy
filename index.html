Privacy Policy for DriveVault

Last updated: June 2026

DriveVault is a vehicle maintenance and document management application designed to help users track vehicle expenses, fuel records, service history, mileage, and important vehicle documents.

Information We Collect

DriveVault stores information entered by users, including vehicle details, fuel records, service records, odometer readings, and uploaded vehicle documents.

How We Use Information

The information is used solely to provide app functionality, including vehicle tracking, maintenance reminders, expense analysis, and document management.

Data Storage

User data is stored locally on the user's device. DriveVault does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Permissions

The application may request access to device storage, notifications, and files solely for app functionality.

Third-Party Services

DriveVault may use trusted third-party libraries and services required for app functionality. These services operate according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

DriveVault is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, users may contact the developer through the contact information provided on Google Play.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
